I write clean, semantic, standards-based markup that’s both maintainable and efficient.But of course my job is more than just making a site look pretty. The visuals of a site are important but it’s only part of the bigger picture (pun intended). In building a website, I’m also concerned with accessibility - a site should be available to as many people as possible; usability - websites should be intuitive and easy to navigate; responsiveness - with a growing number of web enabled devices, a site should adapt to it’s surrounds; and content - a website should have a core message and communicate that message effectively. I endeavour to combine these factors in every project I work on to ensure a top quality website is delivered.
